About Us

Our Story

Northern Climate is a locally owned and operated HVAC company proudly serving Greater Sudbury and the surrounding communities. We are passionate about one thing: keeping Northern Ontario families comfortable through our harsh winters and warm summers.

With years of hands-on experience in heating, cooling, fireplaces, and water heaters, our team has deep roots in the Sudbury community. We understand the unique demands that Northern Ontario weather places on your home comfort systems -- and we have the expertise to handle every one of them.

From emergency furnace repairs on the coldest January night to full air conditioning installations for those scorching July afternoons, Northern Climate is the name your neighbours trust. We treat every home like our own and every customer like family.

How we work

From the first call to the follow-up — what to expect when you bring us into your home.

01

Free in-home assessment

We come on time, walk the home, listen carefully, and look at what's actually installed — not just what the brochure says.

02

Honest, written quote

Plain-language pricing on paper. We explain the options, we explain the trade-offs, and we don't upsell what you don't need.

03

Clean, on-schedule install

Floors covered, old equipment hauled away, work site left tidier than we found it. Most installs wrap up in a single day.

04

Follow-up & maintenance

We check in after install and offer an annual maintenance visit so the system keeps running the way it should — for years, not just months.

Our Trusted Partners

We partner with the industry's most respected brands to deliver quality products backed by industry-leading warranties.

Authorized Bryant Dealer

As an Authorized Bryant Dealer, we offer the full lineup of Bryant heating and cooling equipment — from high-efficiency furnaces and air conditioners to heat pumps and ductless systems. Our factory-trained technicians ensure every installation meets Bryant's rigorous standards.

Premium Fireplace Partners

We proudly carry Regency and Enviro fireplaces — two of the most trusted names in gas, wood, and pellet fireplaces. Whether you want the warmth of a wood-burning fireplace or the convenience of gas, our team handles every step of installation with care.
