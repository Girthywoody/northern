Fireplaces

Home / Services / Fireplaces

Add Warmth & Ambiance to Your Home

Whether you prefer the convenience of gas or the timeless appeal of a wood-burning fireplace, Northern Climate has you covered. As authorized dealers for Regency and Enviro, we provide expert installation, annual servicing, and reliable repairs to keep your fireplace safe, efficient, and beautiful for years to come.

Gas Fireplace Installation

Modern, efficient gas fireplaces with instant on/off convenience. We install top-rated units from Regency and Enviro with clean, professional gas line connections.

Wood Fireplace Installation

Traditional wood-burning fireplaces and stoves for authentic warmth and ambiance. We handle full installations including hearth pads, chimney systems, and code-compliant venting.

Fireplace Inserts

Upgrade your existing fireplace with a high-efficiency gas or wood insert. Inserts fit directly into your current opening to dramatically improve heat output and energy savings.

Fireplace Service & Cleaning

Annual maintenance for safe, reliable operation. We clean burners, inspect pilot assemblies, check gas connections, and remove creosote buildup from wood-burning systems.

Fireplace Repairs

Diagnosis and repair of all fireplace types including gas valve issues, ignition problems, cracked refractory panels, blower motors, and thermocouple replacements.

Chimney & Venting

Proper venting solutions for safety and performance. We install and repair chimney liners, direct-vent systems, and B-vent configurations to meet all building code requirements.

Our Trusted Partners

We partner with industry-leading fireplace manufacturers so you get the best quality products backed by exceptional warranties.

As an authorized Regency dealer, we offer their full lineup of premium gas, wood, and pellet fireplaces. Regency products are built for lasting performance and come with industry-leading limited lifetime warranties.

Enviro fireplaces combine cutting-edge efficiency with stunning design. As an authorized Enviro dealer, we install and service their complete range of gas and wood products — engineered for exceptional heat output and low energy costs.
