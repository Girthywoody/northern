Keep Your Home Warm All Winter

Greater Sudbury winters are no joke. With temperatures regularly plunging well below freezing, a reliable heating system is not a luxury -- it is a necessity. Northern Climate has been helping Sudbury families stay warm and comfortable for years, providing expert furnace installation, repair, and maintenance you can count on when the cold hits hardest.

As an Authorized Bryant Dealer, we install the industry-leading Bryant Evolution and Preferred series furnaces -- engineered for maximum efficiency, quiet operation, and long-lasting reliability. Whether you need a brand-new high-efficiency furnace, a fast repair on a freezing night, or a garage heater to keep your workshop comfortable, our licensed technicians have you covered.

Our Heating Services

Furnace Installation

Upgrade to a high-efficiency Bryant Evolution or Preferred series furnace. We handle the full installation from sizing to commissioning, ensuring peak performance from day one.

Furnace Repair

Fast, accurate diagnostics on all makes and models. Our experienced technicians identify the problem quickly and get your heat running again as soon as possible.

Furnace Maintenance

Annual tune-ups catch small issues before they become costly breakdowns. Regular maintenance extends furnace life, improves efficiency, and keeps your warranty intact.

Garage Heaters

Keep your garage warm and usable all winter long. Whether it is a workshop, hobby space, or vehicle storage, we install reliable garage heating solutions built for Sudbury cold.

Heat Pumps

Energy-efficient heating and cooling in one system. Heat pumps can significantly reduce your utility bills while keeping your home comfortable year-round.

Emergency Heating

Furnace quit in the middle of the night? We offer 24/7 emergency heating service so you are never left in the cold when you need help the most.
