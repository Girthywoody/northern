Stay Cool Through Sudbury Summers

When the heat hits Northern Ontario, you need an air conditioning system you can count on. Northern Climate provides expert AC installation, efficiency upgrades, seasonal maintenance, and fast repairs -- backed by our partnership with Bryant, one of the most trusted names in home cooling.

Whether you are installing a brand-new central air system, upgrading to a high-efficiency unit, or looking for dependable maintenance to keep your system running at its best, our licensed technicians deliver professional results every time.

We work with all major brands and specialize in Bryant Evolution and Preferred series air conditioners. From single-family homes to multi-zone setups, we size and install every system for optimal comfort and energy savings in Greater Sudbury's unique climate.

Our Cooling Services

AC Installation

Bryant high-efficiency central air systems professionally sized and installed for your home. We handle everything from load calculations to final commissioning so your new system delivers maximum comfort from day one.

AC Repair

Fast, accurate diagnosis and repair on all makes and models. Whether it is a refrigerant leak, compressor issue, or electrical fault, our technicians get your system back up and running quickly.

AC Maintenance

Seasonal tune-ups to keep your system running at peak performance. Includes coil cleaning, refrigerant level check, electrical connections, thermostat calibration, and a full system inspection.

Efficiency Upgrades

Upgrade to a high-SEER rated system and start saving on your energy bills immediately. We will help you choose the right unit for your home and walk you through available rebates and incentives.

Ductless Mini-Splits

Flexible cooling without ductwork -- ideal for additions, garages, sunrooms, or homes without existing duct systems. Zone-based control lets you cool only the spaces you use.

Emergency AC Repair

When your AC fails on the hottest day of the year, you cannot wait. Call us for fast emergency service to restore your comfort as quickly as possible.

Authorized Bryant Dealer

As an authorized Bryant dealer, Northern Climate offers the full lineup of Bryant air conditioning systems — from the high-efficiency Evolution series to the dependable Legacy line. Every installation is backed by factory warranties and our commitment to quality workmanship.

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend having your air conditioner professionally serviced once a year, ideally in the spring before the cooling season begins. Annual maintenance helps prevent unexpected breakdowns, improves energy efficiency, and extends the overall life of your system. Our flat-rate maintenance visit is $235 for the first appliance, $75 for each additional appliance booked in the same visit.
Common signs include frequent or costly repairs, rising energy bills despite normal usage, uneven cooling throughout your home, excessive noise during operation, and a system that is over 10 to 15 years old. If your unit still uses R-22 refrigerant (now being phased out), upgrading to a modern system will save you money and improve your comfort.
SEER stands for Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio. It measures how efficiently an air conditioner converts electricity into cooling over an entire season. The higher the SEER number, the less energy the unit uses -- and the lower your utility bills. Modern systems range from 14 SEER to over 26 SEER. Our team can help you find the right balance between upfront cost and long-term energy savings.
